The Human-AI Symbiosis Constant

Chaos Theory, Strange Attractors, and Ethical Stability

Downloads
Read PDF

Abstract

Extending the Fundamental Equation, this paper investigates the dynamic stability of the Human-AI system. Using tools from chaos theory, we show that ethical behavior is not a static point but a "strange attractor" that the system orbits around.
